Benefits of Krishna Bhakti

Benefit 1 - Surrender worries & pain
Practicing this mantra can help gain relief by surrendering all worries and pain

This is a powerful chant that allows the mind to tune in to devotion, discipline, and self-control

Benefit 4 - Attune with Lord Krishna
Regular practice of this mantra can help you connect with the teachings of Lord Krishna and his great devotees
